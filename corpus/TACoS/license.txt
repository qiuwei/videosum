2. Product

The TACoS corpus consists of the following parts:
 * For 127 Videos of kitchen tasks from the "MPII Composites corpus", there are several natural language descriptions
   of the actions in each video, collected via Amazon Mechanical Turk
 * The descriptions contain timestamps that mark the start and endpoint in the video
 * Overall, the corpus contains about 17334 sentences, in 2206 video descriptions.     
 * Additionally, the corpus contains low-level annotations from the "MPII Composites corpus", and a time-stamp based alignments 
   of the video-descriptions with this low-level annotations.
